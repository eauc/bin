Push-Pull
---------

hg outcoming [-f] [-B NAME] [-b BRANCH] [PATH]
hg push [-f] [-B NAME] [-b BRANCH] [PATH]
hg incoming [-f] [-B NAME] [-b BRANCH] [PATH]
hg pull [-f] [-B NAME] [-b BRANCH] [PATH]
  pull/pull changesets [from unrelated repos] [pull bookmark] [from BRANCH]
hg pull -u
  update to new branch head.
hg recover
  recover from interrupted pull.

Paths
-----
hg paths
hg paths default-push
hg paths default-pull
